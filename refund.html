Return & Refund Policy

At Organic Lemons, we strive to deliver the freshest produce. Due to the perishable nature of our products, we follow this return and refund policy:

  • Returns are not accepted once delivery is completed.
  • If you receive damaged, spoiled, or incorrect items, please contact us within 24 hours.
  • We may offer a refund or a replacement after verifying the issue.
  • To request a refund, share a clear photo of the issue via email or WhatsApp.
